\section{Background}

Influence Island is a mini-game within The Sims Freeplay that rewards winning players with a set of exclusive in-game items. 
To win, players must successively win three stages. To win each stage, players must successively win all rounds within a stage. 

Each round starts with a score of 0. Players select from a set of three actions that increase this score by a random amount.
The "small" action randomly increases the score by an inclusive increment between 1 - 20 and the "large" action randomly 
increases the score by an inclusive increment between 20 - 45. While "small" and "large" actions may be chosen an unlimited number of times within and across rounds and stages,
the "chocolate" action increases the score by an inclusive increment between 2 - 9 and may only be chosen three times per stage.
